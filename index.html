Bharat Electoral Literacy Fest

The Bharat Electoral Literacy Fest is a lively and instructive celebration of democracy's most basic tenet: informed and empowered citizens. This multi-day event brings together professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the general public to delve deep into the heart of political processes and civic involvement.
